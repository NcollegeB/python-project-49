Understand your score

Compare each personal best with a stable reference score. The same score always receives the same ranking, whether ten people play or ten thousand.

What the percentile means

BrainHacker uses a fixed, model-based benchmark for each test. It does not use live player data; it is not measured population data. The model uses a fixed reference accuracy for each of the five difficulty levels and assumes three correct answers advance a level. This is not a scientific population study. A result in the 75th percentile means that score is as high as or higher than about 75 out of 100 outcomes predicted by the BrainHacker model.
